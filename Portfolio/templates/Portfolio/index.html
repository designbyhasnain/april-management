Our Story

I purchased my first house at 19 from an auction. It came with a tenant already. I was a young, single, and a stubborn female so instead of being ripped off by contractors, I opted to do a lot of work myself. I lived with a lot of mistakes, some very dangerous.

Eventually I married a contractor, a driven, intelligent, talented man with an impeccable work ethic trying to get his own small business off the ground. We worked hard together to ensure its success. The landlords that we have come in contact with help us appreciate the renters' point of view.

Empowering and strengthening our community will ensure everyone has a chance at that basic need to be met, whether it's your first place, relying on rental income, or wanting to start a business to support your family. April Management cares for you.
